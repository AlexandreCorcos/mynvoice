Create a distinctive and modern logo for a SaaS product called "MYNVOICE", meaning "MY INVOICE".

The design must visually express the idea that "MYNVOICE" is a fusion of "MY" and "INVOICE".

Core idea:
- The letter "N" should act as a bridge or connector between "MY" and "VOICE"
- The "N" can be stylized to represent:
  - flow
  - document structure
  - connection

Design direction:
- "MY" in coral (#FF6B6B)
- "VOICE" in deep petrol (#0F4C5C)
- The "N" can be a custom shape or slightly abstract

Style:
- Minimal but clever
- Geometric and well-balanced
- Premium SaaS aesthetic (Linear, Vercel-level quality)

Optional symbol:
- The "N" should be strong enough to become the app icon (favicon)

Avoid:
- Literal invoice icons
- Dollar signs
- Complex illustrations

Output:
- Full logo (text + symbol)
- Icon-only version based on the "N"
- Light and dark background versions
