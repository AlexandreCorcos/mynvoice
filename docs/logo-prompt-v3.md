Create a premium, minimal logo for a SaaS product called "MYNVOICE", representing "MY INVOICE".

The design should subtly suggest the missing letter "I" between "MY" and "NVOICE".

Concept:
- The logo should visually imply "MY INVOICE" without explicitly writing it
- Use spacing, alignment, or a subtle vertical element to suggest the hidden "I"

Design direction:
- Clean typography
- Modern geometric sans-serif
- Strong attention to spacing and composition

Colors:
- Petrol tones (#0F4C5C, #2C7A7B)
- Coral accent (#FF6B6B) used minimally

Style:
- Very minimal
- Elegant and high-end
- Similar level of refinement as brands like Stripe or Linear

Avoid:
- Obvious tricks
- Over-design
- Decorative elements

Goal:
- A logo that feels intelligent and intentional
- Recognizable even without explanation

Output:
- Logo on light background
- Logo on dark background
- Close-up version highlighting the hidden detail
